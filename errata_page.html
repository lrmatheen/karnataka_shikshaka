The Karnataka Shikshaka app provides Second PUC Computer Science study material for the benefit of both teachers and students.

While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, human errors are possible, particularly in MCQs and other content.

This page hosts a list of reported errors and their corrections. You can contribute by reporting any errors you find. Corrections will be reviewed and published here as soon as possible.

In future app updates, these corrections will be incorporated, and the resolved items will be removed from this list. Together, we can ensure the highest quality learning material for everyone.
